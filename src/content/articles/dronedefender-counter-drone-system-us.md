DroneDefender, a portable counter-drone system developed by Dedrone. Utilizing radio-frequency (RF) jamming, DroneDefender provides a non-kinetic method for neutralizing unmanned aerial threats, primarily commercial drones.

## **System Overview**

- **Name:** DroneDefender

- **Manufacturer:** Dedrone

- **Country of Origin:** United States

- **Type:** RF jammer (electronic countermeasure)

- **Intended Use:** Law enforcement, military, and critical infrastructure protection

## **Technical Specifications**

- **Detection Capabilities:**

Integrated with Dedrone’s AI-driven detection and tracking software

- Can be used with radar, radio-frequency sensors, and electro-optical systems

- **Neutralization Method:**

Radio-frequency (RF) jamming disrupts drone communication and GPS signals

- Forces drones to land or return to their point of origin

- **Effective Range:**

Up to 2 km, depending on environmental conditions and drone type

- **Mobility:**

Man-portable (rifle-like design)

- **Deployment Mode:**

Handheld, battery-powered system

## **Operational Use & Effectiveness**

- **Known Deployments:**

Used by U.S. and allied military forces, law enforcement, and government agencies

- Deployed at high-security events, including U.S. presidential inaugurations

- **Combat/Field Testing History:**

Successfully tested against commercial drones in real-world conditions

- Used in counter-terrorism and border security operations

- **Effectiveness Against Various Drone Types:**

Effective against most commercial and consumer drones

- Limited effectiveness against military-grade UAVs with encrypted communication

## **Notable Features & Advantages**

- **Key Strengths:**

Non-destructive method for disabling drones

- Silent operation without visible or audible detection

- **Unique Capabilities:**

Disrupts multiple communication bands simultaneously (GPS, Wi-Fi, etc.)

- Compact and easy to deploy in urban and field environments

- **Adaptability & Scalability:**

Can be used as part of a larger counter-drone system with multiple sensors

## **Limitations & Challenges**

- **Known Weaknesses:**

Not effective against drones with pre-programmed flight paths

- Limited range compared to kinetic or directed energy solutions

- **Operational Constraints:**

Requires trained operators for effective use

- Battery life limits operational duration

- **Adversary Countermeasures:**

Advanced drones may use frequency-hopping or encrypted communication to resist jamming

- Some UAVs may switch to autonomous operation if jamming is detected

## **Future Developments & Upgrades**

- **Planned Enhancements:**

Research into increased range and frequency coverage

- AI-driven adaptive jamming for enhanced effectiveness

- **Integration with Other Systems:**

Compatible with Dedrone’s full counter-drone ecosystem

- **Future Deployment Plans:**

Expected to see continued expansion in military, law enforcement, and VIP protection roles
