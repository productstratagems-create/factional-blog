## **Norway has formally requested a proposal for the Patriot missile defense system from the United States.**

Norway has formally requested a proposal for the Patriot missile defense system from the United States. The Norwegian Defence Materiel Agency is also considering offers from other allied nations. This is to ensure they make the best decision for their defense needs by 2029.

The Norwegian Defence Materiel Agency (NDMA) has formally requested a proposal from the United States for the Patriot system, including pricing, delivery times, and equipment details.

The NDMA is also considering offers from other allied nations with similar systems.

The inquiry to the United States will provide Norway with a better basis for making a decision on the most suitable solution. The US Patriot system is one of the candidates, but the NDMA is also considering other options. The goal is to have new batteries in place by 2029.
